Customer Questions & Answers

Is there any latency when watching videos?

Asked by James P. on April 5, 2023
AudioTech Support • Official Response

The Pro X7 uses aptX Low Latency codec when available, which reduces latency to under 40ms - virtually imperceptible when watching videos. For devices that don't support aptX LL, standard Bluetooth latency is about 150-200ms which some users might notice.
